HOT YOGA CLASSES

Be prepared to sweat.
Bring a towel and drink plenty of water BEFORE attending.

BEGINNER HOT YOGA

Beginner Hot Yoga classes are taught in a moist, heated room, which helps loosen tight muscles and promotes sweating to help detoxify the body. Each yoga posture is held for 10-60 seconds with a short rest between to relax, integrate, and slow the heart rate. Foundational breathing techniques and alignment principles are taught, slowly and compassionately, with detailed instruction. Variations are offered to accomodate students that might find something too difficult, as well as to more advanced students that want more of a challenge. This class is transformational, will create radiant health, and build flexibility and strength for your body mind and spirit.

VINYASA

Vinyasa classes move at a more energetic pace where students will learn to flow from one yoga posture into the next in conjunction with the breath. Students can look forward to invigorating calestetics, challenging sequences, advanced variations and inversions being introduced. Techniques from various yoga styles including Dharma Yoga, Ashtanga Yoga, Bikram Yoga and Baptiste Power Yoga are integrated in to the teacher's own creative flow. Ideal for advanced beginners ready for more of a challenge.

SILENT HOT YOGA

The 7am Silent classes are practiced with very little verbal instuction. Students should attend our regular Beginner Hot Yoga classes prior to attending.

NON-HEATED CLASSES

KIDS YOGA

Kids Yoga classes are intended for children ages 5-9, and runs in sessions on Saturday mornings at 10am, in conjunction with our 10am Beginner Hot Yoga class. Parents can attend the 10am Beginner Hot Yoga class while their children are in session.

BEGINNER BASICS

Every Other Sunday at Noon

Our Beginner Basics class is great for students new to yoga as well as seasoned practitioners seeking gentler, less challenging class. Students are taught basic poses, breathing and meditation techniques to help keep their body and mind healthy and prepare them for more advanced practices. Bring your family and friends and share the healing benefits of yoga together.

RESTORATIVE YOGA

First Sunday of every month, from 12:30-2pm

Restorative Yoga is offered on the first Sunday of each month, from 12:30-2pm. This is a deeply relaxing yoga class, where postures are supported by props (pillows, blankets) enabling the student to fully relax and meditate, bringing the nervous system and spirit back to rest.

KUNDALINI YOGA

Third Sunday of every month, from 12:00-1:15pm

Awaken your chakras and heighten your spirits! Kundalini Yoga is the yoga of awareness, aiming to cultivate the creative spiritual potential of a human to uphold values, speak truth, and focus on the compassion and consciousness needed to serve and heal others.

DIPAMKARA MEDITATION

Every Sunday, 7pm-8:30pm with Buddhist teacher Todd Drexler

Eight Steps to Happiness by Geshe Kelsang Gyatso explains eight beautiful verses that comprise one of Buddhism's best-loved and most enduring teachings. This short text reveals profound yet totally practical methods to enable a powerful awakening of the heart, the one true source of all of our happiness. Drop in to any class. $5 SHY members $10 non-members.
